There's lots of data surrounding this election and past elections. But what does it all mean?

Visualisations can be an extremely helpful tool for exploring and understanding data. They can help us find the diamond information in the data rough.

This website is my own way of exploring the data surrounding Australia's Federal Elections. However, with any luck I can help others discover more about their own country, electorates or (heaven forbid) inform some opinions.

There's not much content here to start off with. Just preferences diagrams for each electorate in 2010. However, I hope to add more years of data, more features like browsing electorates by map or viewing by polling station and different types of visualisations such as electorate swing over time.
